Instant Noodles

Description:

Instant noodles are a quick, pre-cooked meal made from wheat flour, often accompanied by a seasoning packet. It's easy to make and tasty to eat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of instant-noodles
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • sliced vegetables (carrot, tomato etc)(optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil water and put the packet of instant-noodles in it.
  2. Stir it for 2-3 minutes and keep aside
  3. Cook the sliced vegetables in another pan and put the egg in it with the seasonings that came with the packet of instant noodles.
  4. Cook for 6-7 minutes and put the boiled noodles in it.
  5. Stir it for 1-2 minutes and put out the flame.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
