Ginger-Tea

Description:

Ginger tea is a soothing and aromatic herbal drink known for its digestive and anti-inflammatory properties. It helps alleviate nausea, improve circulation, and boost immunity.

Ingredients:

  • Peeled and sliced ginger.
  • 1.5 cups of water.
  • 1-2 spoon sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tea-spoon lemon extract.

Steps:

  1. Boil water and add the ginger in it and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Strain the tea in a cup, add honey/sugar and the lemon extract for more flavour.
  3. Enjoy!
